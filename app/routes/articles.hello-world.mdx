## Overview

One Paradox is a first-person narrative adventure set in a dystopian future. Playing as an android on your first day at the OF-1 factory, you move from room to room completing assigned tasks — until the cracks start to show.

Originally developed as a final degree project at Institut Obert de Catalunya, the game was later submitted to the **Fuck Capitalism Jam 2024** and subsequently published on Steam as a free-to-play title through **32 Bits Studio**.

## My Role

Lead Developer on a six-person team. Responsible for the full production pipeline alongside hands-on work across every technical and creative discipline.

## Contributions

**Production & Design** — Coordinated the team, managed scope, and led the overall design of the experience, from game flow to puzzle structure.

**Programming** — Implemented core gameplay systems including interaction logic, puzzle mechanics, and android behaviour.

**Lighting** — Built and iterated on the lighting setup to establish the synthwave/retro-futuristic atmosphere central to the game's identity.

**UI** — Designed and implemented the full user interface.

**Soundtrack & FX** — Composed and integrated the retrowave soundtrack and sound effects.

**Voice Acting & Dubbing** — Coordinated and participated in the full Catalan voice dubbing, the game's primary audio language.

**QA** — Led the quality assurance process through to release.

## Release

Published on **Steam** (Sep 30, 2024) and **itch.io** as a free-to-play title. Available in English, Spanish, and Catalan.
